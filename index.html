I am a doctoral student in mathematics at the University of Sherbrooke under the supervision of Jean-Philippe Burelle and Maxence Mayrand.

My area of research is symplectic geometry, complex geometry, higher Teichmüller theory and topological quantum field theories. I'm particularly interested in deformation spaces of geometric structures and their relations to Poisson geometry and topological quantum field theory.
